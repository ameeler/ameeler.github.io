About Me

Hello!! My name is Alex and I am a junior Computer Engineering student at UT Austin!

I am an aspiring application developer who also has strong interests in data science and machine learning. I have worked with .NET and React through my internship and personal projects. I am also a Co-Founder and CEO of a company called Univvfy. I created the company with several other partners with the goal of building software that helps university students find events around their campuses. It also gives university and student-led organizations online tools to help them run their organizations and advertise their events to students.

Music plays a huge role in my personal life. I am a self-taught musician and have been playing drums and other instruments for around 7 years. In August of 2021, a few friends and I created the Austin-based band “Yardsale!” We have been creating sweet tunes and playing around Austin and UT and even have opened for the popular indie-pop band “COIN”.

Language learning is another large aspect of my personal life. I am currently learning Japanese with the goal of learning as many languages as I can in my lifetime.

I am a very passionate and driven person who will achieve anything I set my mind to. I am definitely a very talkative and friendly person who prefers a very hands-on team-oriented environment.
